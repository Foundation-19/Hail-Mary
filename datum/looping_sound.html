get_soundConverts SOUND_LOOP_ENTRY('sound/file.ogg', length, weight) to list('sound/file.ogg', length) and returns that
playtakes in list('sound/file.ogg', mid_length) and plays the file and ques up another in mid_length deciseconds
stop*Kill kills the timer, even if things are still in the output atoms list This is crucial for things like microwaves and weather, as they'll always have something in their list, and otherwise it'll NEVER stop playing Areas use output_atoms differently, as a list of players to play the sound to. If we stop it as normal and kill it, itll stop playing to all mobs who were listening! To sum up: If the output_atoms are playing the sound, kill should = true If the output_atoms are listening to the sound, kill should = false
